What's on

First announcements for the Arts Festival 2025.

We want to make sure you never miss out on important details, updates, and special offers related to our Festival programming and other activities.

Christmas Market Festival

For Christmas 2024, the City of the Tribes will shine with the glorious with Galway Christmas Market runs from Friday, November 8th,2024 to Sunday, January 5th, 2025.

Irish Dancing Festival 2025

Galway Dance Festival, also known as Traditional Dance Festival Ireland, is held at Festival House, 12 East Essex Street, Temple Bar, Galway, Ireland. This year's festivities begin on July 29th to August 1st, 2025.  

Block Rockin Beats 2025

Dec Pierce's Block Rockin Beats is a DJ set with a full live band that performs the biggest dance tunes in the world, fusing rock and rave. After one of the most unforgettable performances of this year's Festival, they will return to the Heineken® Big Top on July 18, 2025 in Galway City Centre.

Imagine Arts Festival 2025

Waterford will once again be the center of culture and innovation from March 22–27, 2025, when the Imagine Arts Festival returns. There is something for everyone at this year's event, whether you've been a fan for a long time or are just visiting. It promises to be a lively celebration of music, performance, visual arts, and heritage.
